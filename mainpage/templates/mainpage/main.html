ABOUT ME

I am a self-taught software engineer and I am looking for opportunities to expand my knowledge and work in this field. I am currently working as an account manager at an IT sales company. This experience has really opened my eyes to the world of technology, I think it is amazing how software has become a part of every industry, business, and for every function imaginable.

Programming started out as a hobby, but has quickly escalated into a passion. This is due to the vast amount of materials online and on local bookshelves, which made it easy for me to learn and get excited about the field. I have especially enjoyed solving algorithm problems. Each problem is a puzzle and I enjoy the careful process of arriving to an answer.

I have been diligent in learning on my own, but I find my conversations with other engineers to be the most exciting. That is why I am looking forward to work in a team setting, to be a part of an environment where I can collaborate and learn.
